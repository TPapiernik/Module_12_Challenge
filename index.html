Hello Volunteers! This dashboard is here to assist you, and all the other volunteers who are part of this exciting project. Using the dropdown box below, you can select your Test Subject ID Number to view the key data about the sample you submitted for our study. If you are curious, you can also explore the results from the other anonymous volunteers participating in the study. As time goes on, if Bacterial Species of Interest are identified by Improvable Beef, you will be able to easily check if you are a candidate for further study.
